America's Crumbling Infrastructure

Welcome! This page is dedicated to raising the awareness of a serious and potentially deadly threat to America. The problem may seem insignificant, but it affects almost every person in America, if not every person. The problem is infrastructure. You may picture infrastructure as superhighways, skyscrapers, and massive bridges, so you may think it’s not a big deal if they are worn out (especially if you live in a rural area). The word “infrastructure” covers a multitude of structures and buildings, beyond what is normally thought of when thinking of the word. Infrastructure includes areas such as water sanitation, public transit, energy production plants, and even public schools. Whether you live in a bustling city, or in a roaming rural land, this problem will affect you. As the title suggests, America's infrastructure is old and decrepit, and every day it continues to get worse and worse. The problem will eventually reach a catastrophic point where a tragedy will occur. People will look back upon it and realize more should have been done, but it will be too late. If you take the time to go through my website, you can learn more about this issue and how you can help.
